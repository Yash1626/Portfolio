About Me

My journey in technology began amidst the foothills of the Himalayas and has led me to pursue a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science Engineering, where the binary world of computers fascinates me every single day. Beyond the confines of academia, I don the hat of a culinary wizard at home, a scribe of thoughts in solitude, and a beacon of knowledge as a tutor to those seeking enlightenment. My soul finds solace in the art of creation, be it delectable cuisines or weaving words that resonate. In the tapestry of life, I am but a thread interwoven with the vibrant hues of Indian culture, where camaraderie is forged over a game of cricket in gullies, and wisdom is shared under the banyan tree.
